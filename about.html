About Ansari Interiors

15+ Years of Trusted Interior Design in Chennai

Our Story

Founded in 2012, Ansari Interiors began with a simple vision: to transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary homes. What started as a small design studio in Anna Nagar has grown into one of Chennai's most trusted interior design companies.

Over the past 15+ years, we've had the privilege of working with hundreds of families across Chennai – from cozy apartments in T. Nagar to luxurious villas in OMR. Each project has taught us something new, and every client has become part of our extended family.

Our journey has been marked by continuous learning, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to quality. We've evolved from traditional interior design to embracing modern technologies and sustainable practices, always keeping our clients' dreams at the heart of everything we do.
